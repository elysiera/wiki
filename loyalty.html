Loyalty Points

As you embark on your adventures in the realm, you have the opportunity to earn loyalty points, which can elevate your gameplay experience. Every day, you'll automatically receive 3 loyalty points, simply for being an active player. For those who choose to upgrade to a VIP account, the rewards are even greater, with an additional 3 loyalty points awarded daily, totaling 6 loyalty points per day.

In addition to the daily loyalty points, VIP players enjoy an exclusive benefit. When purchasing VIP time, you'll receive an immediate boost of 6 loyalty points for each day purchased. This means that as a VIP, you can rapidly accumulate loyalty points and progress through the Loyalty Ranks at an accelerated pace.

Accumulating loyalty points over time allows you to progress through our prestigious Loyalty Ranks. These ranks bestow skill bonuses that enhance your abilities and make you a formidable force in the realm. As you climb the ranks, your skill bonus increases, providing you with a significant advantage during battles and challenges.

From the Novice of Elysiera to the esteemed Supreme of Elysiera, each rank represents a milestone in your journey. As you attain higher ranks, the skill bonuses become more substantial, enabling you to unleash your true potential. The loyalty points you earn serve as a testament to your dedication and commitment to the realm.
