Overview

The Item Exchange System offers players an innovative approach to item transformation. By amalgamating three items from a designated group, players can obtain a different item from the same group. This system serves to refresh and diversify a player's inventory, promoting the strategic exchange of less desired items to potentially secure more favorable ones.

The Item Exchange building is located to the west of the adveunturer's guild, beyond a bridge.

Process

  1. Item Grouping: To initiate the exchange, players must gather three items belonging to the same group.
  2. Item Variation: The system guarantees that the resultant item will differ from the three items traded. To enhance the probability of receiving a desired item, players are encouraged to trade three distinct items they no longer want.
  3. Gold Cost: A significant gold fee is associated with the exchange, varying based on the item group in question.
