Elysiera has a unique way to refill your hunts, or even completely swap supply loads with loadouts. When you type !loadout in game you'll see this:

Loadouts can be created by editing one !loadout edit energy or !loadout edit bosses, the loadout name can be anything! If you ever forget a command don't forget you can always hit !loadout to see example usage.

Configuring Loadouts

When doing !loadout edit you'll have the opportunity to configure your loadout.

Note that only items that are sold by Battlemart and Stashable (meaning can be stored in your stash) are available for loadouts. You have to use the full name of the item (no abbreviations).

Any lines prefixed with a + will add items to your loadout, followed by the item name and then a number of items to acquire. If you have more than that number, the rest will be stashed when accepting the loadout from Battlemart.

Any lines prefixed with a - will remove items. Meaning these items will be stashed when filling a loadout from Battlemart, all of them.

The system will tell which lines are invalid when you submit a loadout, so you can try some things out to get the hang of it.

Filling Loadouts

Once you have loadouts setup (you can see them via !loadout list). You can ask Battlemart to fill them for you. This process is really simple. When you greet them, they will offer you to trade or to fill a loadout. Click loadout (or simply say loadout) and Battlemart will list all of your available loadout configurations. Once you select one this will happen:

  • All undesired items (items configured with a - ) will be sent you your Stash.
  • For each desired item, Battlemart will pull from your Stash into your inventory, free of charge to you. If this is enough to fill your order, you're done!
  • Now, Battlemart will gladly sell you all the remaining items you need, and once that's done, assuming you have enough money and capacity, you're ready for battle!
