Players can do up to 2 tasks a day (3 if VIP), they can be started using the task tracker in your inventory.

Kill Tasks

Kill tasks are timed trials, you have 30 minutes to kill as many of a certain creature as you can. There are a few task tiers:

In order to complete each task, you need to achieve one of the 4 ranks: commoner, journeyman, epic, legendary. Most tasks follow the same patterns, respectively:

  • A-Tier: 350, 500, 650, 800 kills
  • B-Tier: 300, 400, 500, 600 kills
  • C-Tier: 200, 250, 300, 400 kills
  • D-Tier: 150, 200, 250, 350 kills

And in turn, each rank will give you a boosted gold and experience reward:

  • Commoner: Experience 1✕, Gold: 1✕
  • Journeyman: Experience 1.2✕, Gold: 1.1✕
  • Epic: Experience 1.4✕, Gold: 1.2✕
  • Legendary: Experience 1.6✕, Gold: 1.3✕

Party

When hunting in a party, the requirements change a bit. The formula is requiredKills * partySize * 0.7. So for example:

2 people, C-tier, commoner: 200 * 2 * 0.7 = 280
4 people, A-tier, legendary: 800 * 4 * 0.7 = 2240

Hazard System

Some tasks are you the hazard system, this means that if you hit legendary rank on them, you'll be able to increase your hazard level (and reset your task for the day, enabling you to take the task again). Having a higher hazard level increases loot chances and experience gained, but makes the creatures more challenging. The hazard level also enables a small chance of a custom mini boss to spawn, this boss has guaranteed loot, for example, the Falcon boss will always drop a piece falcon gear.

You'll find an NPC to control the hazard level for each of these close to their hunting grounds.
