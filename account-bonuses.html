You'll now receive bonuses for the highest level of each vocation on your account, and you'll also be able to login to multiple characters at once. Note, though, that only one character can be outside of a protection zone at a time, this is only meant to allow you to train multiple characters. The initial bonuses are as follows; this feature is currently experimental and we'll adjust these numbers in the coming days:

  • highest level char: 0.06 speed per level
  • highest level knight: +0.005% mitigation per level
  • highest level paladin: +0.075 dexterity levels per level
  • highest level sorcerer: +0.0025% critical hit chance per level
  • highest level druid: +0.005% healing per level
  • highest level rookstayer: +0.3 capacity per level

You'll stop accruing bonuses at level 1500.
