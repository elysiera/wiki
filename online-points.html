While playing, you're constantly gaining Online Points. These points can be used in the in-game store to buy some useful gear or boosts.

You can explore the in-game store to see what you can buy. Enjoy!

  • VIP Players: Gain 100 online points per hour
  • Regular Players: Gain 50 online points per hour

Points are awarded in batches of 50, so as a VIP you'll gain 50 coins every 30 minutes! You don't need to worry about when you logged in our out, we keep track of the minutes for you so you don't have to try and min-max it :)
